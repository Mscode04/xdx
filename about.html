AwaazNet Initiative

Breaking communication barriers with AI

The Problem

  • Lack of continuous monitoring and feedback for speech therapy
  • Limited access to personalized exercises
  • Delayed intervention for speech challenges
  • Social anxiety and reduced confidence
  • Difficulty with pronunciation in language learning

Our Solution

Continuous Voice Analysis

Real-time monitoring of speech patterns and disfluencies

Personalized Exercises

Tailored activities based on individual speech patterns

Multilingual Support

Currently supporting English and Malayalam with more to come

Progress Tracking

Detailed analytics to monitor improvement over time

We envision a future where communication barriers no longer exist—where every voice, regardless of its challenges, is heard clearly and confidently.

Our Impact

Technology with purpose

Smart Care App

Developed in collaboration with Makkaraparamba Palliative Care, this intuitive solution supports caregivers and medical professionals in managing patients more effectively, enhancing quality of life for those in need.

The app's unveiling at the 'Arike' event was graced by Mr. Muhammad Musthafa and Dr. P. Unneen, underscoring its significance in healthcare innovation.

Educational Transformation

We've built digital infrastructures for schools that revolutionize learning experiences, making education more accessible and engaging through technology.

Our platforms enable seamless communication between students, teachers, and parents while providing analytics to improve educational outcomes.
